MT ARJUNO

Mount Arjuno's climbing trails are renowned for their extreme nature among hikers. Located within Bogor and Sukabumi Regencies, Mount Arjuno is an attractive hiking destination for nature enthusiasts.

Reaching a height of 2,211 meters above sea level, Mount Arjuno offers unique natural beauty and challenges that test the courage of climbers. Known for its high difficulty level, Mount Arjuno has dense and often closed forest vegetation.

Weather conditions in this area also tend to be unpredictable. The trails are steep, slippery, and traverse the mountain ridges.
